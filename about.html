Temperature Based Weather Guide

This table explains how weather conditions are generally categorized based on temperature ranges. The percentages represent approximate frequency of these weather conditions globally.

Weather Type Temperature Range Icon Approx Occurence Description
☀️ Sunny/Hot Above 30°C 30% High temperature with strong sunlight and clear sky. Common in summer and tropical regions.
⛅ Partly Cloudy 20°C - 30°C 35% Moderate temperature with partial cloud cover. Comfortable weather conditions
🌧️ Rainy 15°C - 25°C 20% Moist air conditions causing rainfall. Common during monsoon or storm systems.
🌬️ Windy 10°C - 20°C 10% Moderate temperature with strong air movement. Usually caused by pressure differences in atmosphere.
❄️ Cold Below 10°C 10% Low temperature conditions. In colder regions it may include snow or frost.
